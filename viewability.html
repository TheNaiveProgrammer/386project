Ability Effect

Flash Fire makes the Pokémon immune to Fire-type moves and will activate when hit by one. When activated, the power of the Pokémon's Fire-type moves is increased by 50%. While subsequent hits by Fire-type moves will not provide further increases in power, the Pokémon remains immune to the moves. Flash Fire will not activate if the Pokémon is protected from the Fire-type move. Flash Fire will not work while the user is frozen.
